Tic Tac Toe

Welcome to Tic Tac Toe! The object of the game is simple. Create a row of 3 (horizontally, vetically, or diagonally) before your opponent. You will take turns placing pieces on the board and cannot move a piece once it has been played. The game is a draw if there are no open squares on the board. If you want to reset the game, please press the Reset button.
